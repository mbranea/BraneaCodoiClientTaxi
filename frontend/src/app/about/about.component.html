A little bit about our history:

Brasov Taxi Cab Company was founded in 1978 by local cab drivers. It is one the oldest cab companies in Brasov. Even though we are not one of the largest cab companies, we have stayed prosperous in business by offering our clients a variety of options that other cab companies don't, such as: reservations as far off ahead of time as you like, confirmation of trips, email receipts through SQUARE, unbeatable discounts and flat rates. We have always been and still are happy to establish company contract agreements in order to transport company employees and medical transports all over the city.
